  • Service cancellations within the stipulated period.
  • Non-delivery of services due to unforeseen circumstances.

To initiate a refund, please contact us at technobuzzsystems@gmail.com or call us at 7066666694 within 7 days of the transaction date. Provide the following details:

  • Transaction ID or Payment Reference Number

  • Reason for the refund request

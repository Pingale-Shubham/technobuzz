Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Technobuzz Systems, we prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to deliver top-notch software solutions. We understand that there may be instances where a refund or cancellation is necessary. This policy outlines the procedures and conditions under which refunds and cancellations are processed for payments made through Razorpay.

1. Refund Eligibility

  • Duplicate payments.
  • Accidental overpayments.
  • Service cancellations within the stipulated period.
  • Non-delivery of services due to unforeseen circumstances.

To initiate a refund, please contact us at technobuzzsystems@gmail.com or call us at 7066666694 within 7 days of the transaction date. Provide the following details:

  • Transaction ID or Payment Reference Number
  • Date and amount of the transaction
  • Reason for the refund request

2. Refund Processing

Upon receiving a valid refund request:

  • We will review the request and notify you of the approval or rejection.
  • Approved refunds will be processed using Razorpay's refund mechanisms.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.
Refund Timelines:
  • Normal Refunds: Processed within 40-45 business days.
  • Instant Refunds: Processed within minutes, subject to eligibility and payment method.

Note: Instant Refunds may incur a nominal fee, as per Razorpay's policies.

3. Cancellations

  • Notify us at least 48 hours before the scheduled service delivery.
  • Cancellations made within this timeframe are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations after this period may not be eligible for a refund depending on the service agreement.

4. Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Change of mind after service delivery.
  • Partial use of services.
  • Delays caused by third-party service providers or events beyond our control.
